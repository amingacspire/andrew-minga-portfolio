Email Security Checker

Checks a domain's SPF, DMARC, DKIM, and MX records live and explains what the results mean in plain language. Read-only DNS lookups via Cloudflare's public resolver; nothing is stored, logged, or sent anywhere else.

No selector? Over 30 well-known selectors (Microsoft 365, Google, SendGrid, Mailchimp, Postmark, Fastmail, and more) are probed automatically.

Results for

Read the fine print: a missing DKIM result on the default selectors is not conclusive; the domain may sign with a custom selector. DMARC p=none is monitoring, not protection. Third-party senders (marketing platforms, ticketing systems) each need their own SPF include and DKIM selector, so one passing check does not mean every mail stream is covered.
