DNS Propagation Check

Queries the same record across Cloudflare, Google, and Quad9 at once. Matching answers mean propagation is complete; differences mean a record changed recently or a resolver is serving a stale cache.

Public resolvers only reveal their own cached view, not every recursive resolver worldwide, so this is a strong signal of propagation, not a global guarantee. DNSSEC-validated answers are marked.
